### 🚀 Featured Projects

#### 🧠 [Disease Detection Application](https://github.com/Msubasi1/Disease-Detection-Application-using-Machine-Learning)
A Streamlit web app that predicts the likelihood of four different diseases from patient-questionnaire data. SelectKBest (chi²) feature selection + Logistic Regression, reaching **~97% accuracy** on the held-out test set.
`Python` · `scikit-learn` · `Streamlit` · `pandas`

#### 🔬 [Skin Cancer Detection by Lesion Images](https://github.com/Msubasi1/Skin-Cancer-Detection-by-Lesion-Images-with-ML)
A Convolutional Neural Network that classifies seven types of pigmented skin lesions from the HAM10000 dataset. Built and tuned (batch size, epochs, learning rate) with Keras, hitting **~75% test accuracy** with a strong AUC.
`Python` · `TensorFlow / Keras` · `scikit-learn` · `pandas`

#### 🎵 [Geographical Origin of Music](https://github.com/Msubasi1/Geographical-Origin-Of-Music)
Predicts the country of origin of a music track from its low-level audio features. Compares Random Forest, Logistic Regression, SVM, and an MLP regressor — with GeoPandas-backed world-map visualizations.
`Python` · `scikit-learn` · `GeoPandas` · `matplotlib`
